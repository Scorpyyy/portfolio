What do I need to put my website online?

Just two simple things — and don't worry, I'll set up both for you. Here they are, in plain language.

Your Domain Name

A domain is simply your website's address on the internet — what people type to find you. Every business needs its own unique address so customers can reach you easily and remember you.

yourbusiness.ma

Renewed once a year.

Hosting

Hosting is the space where your website lives. It stores your site and keeps it online 24 hours a day, so customers can visit anytime — day or night.

online 24/7

Renewed monthly or yearly, depending on your plan.

Imagine your website is a house. The domain is its address. Hosting is the land the house is built on.

Why aren't domain & hosting included?

Because they're services provided by specialized companies — a little like paying for electricity or internet at a physical shop. Keeping them in your own name means you:

  • Own your website completely
  • Keep full control
  • Renew whenever you choose
  • Move to another provider anytime
And don't worry about the technical side — I'll personally guide you through buying your domain and hosting, and set everything up for you. You don't need any technical knowledge at all.
